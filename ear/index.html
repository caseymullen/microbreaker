Ear Tuner
ear training for musicians

Ear Tuner measures your aural acuity — your ability to tell which of two notes is slightly higher or lower — and helps you improve it. The app tests you with notes that are closer and closer together, until you can no longer reliably tell which is higher.

The app remembers your best score for every note. Once you have a score for every note, it randomly selects notes to re-test, favoring notes where your score is weakest. You can manually re-test a specific note in the Scores table, below.

By practicing regularly, your aural acuity will improve.

Decrease the number of tests per round (in Settings) to find your initial level faster — though fewer tests means more luck. Increase the number of tests per round to improve accuracy.

Ear Tuner was inspired by Molly Gebrian's tips for improving intonation.
